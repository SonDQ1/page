DX-Ball is a fairly well known Arkanoid game created by Michael P. Welch. in 1996 and rewritten by the BorodinART studio in JavaScript in 2012. As in all arkanoid games, the task is simple: you need to hit the ball by moving the platform in such a way that the ball hits colorful blocks. After destroying all the blocks on the screen, the level changes to the next, in total there are 50 different levels in the game.

How to Play

When passing the game, the score is kept, in the upper left corner of the screen, at the end of the game your record will be saved in the general table of achievements, for the convenience of the game, you can expand the game to full screen by pressing the f11 key, turn off the sound, press the S key, We wish you a pleasant game!
